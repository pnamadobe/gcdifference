The Grand Circle Difference

Best value in travel—guaranteed

There is so much included in your trip price, such as all house wine, house beer, and soft drinks aboard our award-winning river ships.

You will always receive an unbeatable value when you choose Grand Circle Cruise Line and Grand Circle Travel. Your trip price includes a series of guided tours and Exclusive Discovery Series events to connect you with the culture of your destination, as well as all accommodations; most meals—and all onboard house beer, house wine, and soft drinks on our privately-owned river ships—and gratuities for local guides and motorcoach drivers. We guarantee that you won’t find a lower price—if you do, we’ll match it.

Our local Program Directors

Our local Program Directors give you insight into the culture of your destination as only an insider can.

Your group of 38 travelers (on average; one of 3 to 4 groups total) on River Cruises and group of 30 travelers (on average) on Land Vacations will be accompanied by one of our local Program Directors. These resident experts will connect you with the people and culture of the places you’re exploring. Plus, with more than 2,000 regional associates and 35 offices worldwide, enjoy the comfort of knowing our team is always available, 24/7.

Unique experiences & cultural connections

We provide opportunities to interact with locals allowing an inside look at daily life.

We take you into the heart of your destination’s culture, providing you with a glimpse of its people’s day-to-day lives. With our Exclusive Discovery Series events, like Home-Hosted meals and visits to local schools through Grand Circle Foundation, you’ll learn about the local culture from those who work, learn, and live there.

Leader in solo travel

See why solo travelers choose Grand Circle as their trusted travel partner.

We believe you shouldn’t have to pay a premium to explore the world the way you want, so we offer FREE Single Supplements on all Land Vacations, low-cost Single Supplements on all River Cruises, and FREE Single Supplements on all pre- or post-extensions. Whether you’re traveling independently, or sharing your discoveries with a close friend or family member, the natural camaraderie of your group will ensure that you’ll never feel alone. And with your Program Director at your side and regional Grand Circle offices nearby for support, you’ll feel safe and secure, too.

Customize your trip

There are so many ways to customize your trip to create a unique travel experience that’s just right for you.

Many travelers are choosing to make their vacations their own—in fact, 87% of our travelers now take advantage of the freedom and flexibility to customize their trip. Spend more time in the region by arriving early or adding pre- and post-trip extensions, choose your preferred airline, departure city, upgrade options, and more.

American travelers

Enjoy camaraderie with American travelers who share your passion for discovery.

Since 1958, we have been crafting itineraries for active American travelers—typically over the age of 50—who are young at heart and interested in experiencing a destination—not just seeing it.
